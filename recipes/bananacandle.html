Pineapple and Banana Candle



Description:

The best attempt one can make at creating what looks like a candle with simple foods, using pineapple as a base, banana as the candle, cherry as a flame, and mayonnaise as melting wax.


Ingredients:

    2 Bananas

    2 Tbsp Lemon Juice

    4 Pineapple Rings

    2 Tbsp Mayonnaise

    Orange rind for candle handles

    Maraschino cherries



Method:

    Cut bananas in half (4 pieces) and dip in lemon juice.

    Cut holes in the pineapple rings the same size as the bananas (could use a cookie cutter).

    Arrange the bananas in the pineapple holes.

    Add lemon rind to the bananas with half a toothpick to form the handle of the candle.

    Use the other half of the toothpick to add a cherry on top of the bananas.

    Drizzle mayonnaise over the bananas to simulate melting candle wax.
